Welcome to Our Home

My wife and I warmly welcome you to our home in Ayodhya.

We are proud parents of three wonderful children who have grown up with strong values and big dreams. Our youngest son is a software engineer from IIT Hyderabad. Our elder son is currently pursuing his MBA from IIM Ahmedabad. Our eldest daughter studied at TISS and is now happily working and settled in Bangalore with her family.

While our children are building their journeys across India, my wife and I have found our joy in hosting guests and welcoming people from all walks of life into our home.

We run this Airbnb together with love, care, and personal attention. For us, hosting is not a business — it is seva. We truly enjoy meeting new people, sharing stories, and making sure every guest feels comfortable and at home.

Our property is just about 20 minutes away from the Ram Mandir and other prominent places of Ayodhya, making it a peaceful yet convenient place to stay.

My wife is a true foodie at heart — if you love chaat and local flavors, she will happily guide you to the best places in Ayodhya!

We hope you have a wonderful and peaceful stay with us.
